We are on a mission to make algo-trading accessible to everyone.

Bloom Algo was born from a desire to level the playing field, providing retail traders with the powerful tools once reserved for large institutions.

Our Journey

2021 - The Inception

A group of passionate traders and developers came together with a shared vision: to simplify automated trading for the everyday investor.

2022 - Building the Core

We spent a year developing and backtesting our core engine, focusing on reliability, speed, and user-friendly design.

2023 - Beta Launch

Our platform was launched to a select group of beta testers. Their feedback was instrumental in refining our features.

2024 - Public Launch

Bloom Algo officially launched to the public, offering a suite of powerful tools and strategies for all traders.
